About Us

Jamii Afyaplus Limited (JALI) is a social enterprise producing a range of eco-friendly and cost-effective range of reusable pads that help cater period poverty and address its negative psychosocial consequences for women and girls in Tanzania.

Jamii AfyaPlus Limited promotes a Natural Healthy Lifestyle,restoring the confidence and smiles of thousands of girls and women in rural areas of Tanzania. We provide Menstrual Heath Hygiene training in Schools/communities and have a growing network of rural-based female youth and women trained and economically empowered by Jamii AfyaPlus Limited

Our Mission

To end period poverty, and unemployment by improving the well-being of girls, and women through the production of an affordable range of reusable sanitary pads, training, and awareness on Menstrual Health and Hygiene.

Our Vision

A leading East African Social enterprise producing a range of eco-friendly cost-effective reusable sanitary pads and diapers, reaching the most underserved.
