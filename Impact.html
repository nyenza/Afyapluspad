About Us

Since we began our journey in 2022, Jamii Afyaplus Limited (JALI) has produced and supplied over 37,000 packs of Afyaplus antimicrobial reusable sanitary pads to school-going girls and other individuals in both urban and rural areas.

We have established significant partnerships with several NGOs working in WASH, girls’ programs, and women’s empowerment, and we collaborate with agents across multiple regions of Tanzania. Additionally, through our innovative pad-sewing training, JALI has empowered over 34 girls by teaching them how to make their own reusable sanitary pads and develop sewing skills, helping them overcome poverty and unemployment
